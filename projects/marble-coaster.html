PR-001 — Marble Coaster

← BACK TO DRAWING REGISTER

Design Notes

ROLE: DESIGNER + BUILDER · TOOLS: ONSHAPE, FDM

A marble coaster that never stops: a custom helical lift carries marbles back to the top while a 3D-printed gearbox drives continuous motion. Track curvature and drop heights were calculated to keep the marble moving reliably through every element, run after run.

  1. Helical lift — custom spiral lift returns marbles to the start of the circuit, enabling continuous motion instead of one-shot runs.
  2. 3D-printed gearbox — purpose-built reduction drives the lift; designed for printability and hand assembly.
  3. Track geometry — curvature and drop heights calculated against energy losses so the marble completes the full course reliably.
