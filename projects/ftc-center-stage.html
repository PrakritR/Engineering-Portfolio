Design Notes

ROLE: ROBOT DESIGN + BUILD · TOOLS: ONSHAPE, CNC, FDM

The complete robot architecture for the FTC Center Stage season — drivetrain, intake, outtake and supporting structure — developed under seasonal time constraints. Full mechanical assemblies were modeled to define motion geometry, mounting interfaces and structural load paths before parts were cut.

  1. Outtake + vertical lift — motion geometry defined in CAD to reach scoring positions repeatably within the robot's footprint.
  2. Intake — front intake geometry integrated with the drivetrain and structure without compromising either.
  3. Drivetrain + structure — lightened structural panels carry the load paths; components CNC-machined and 3D-printed, then proven through iterative testing and live competition.
