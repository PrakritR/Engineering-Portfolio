Design Notes

ROLE: ROBOT DESIGN + BUILD · TOOLS: ONSHAPE, CNC, FDM

A full FTC competition robot developed from concept through final integration. Detailed CAD models managed packaging, weight distribution and subsystem interfaces inside competition rule limits; components were CNC-machined and 3D-printed, then refined through assembly testing and match performance.

  1. Roller intake — front intake sized and positioned in CAD so game pieces feed cleanly into the robot within the legal footprint.
  2. Subsystem packaging — drivetrain, intake and outtake interfaces resolved in the full-assembly model — weight distribution managed against rule limits.
  3. Integration + validation — manufactured with CNC and additive processes; integrated and refined through assembly testing and match performance validation.
