Design Notes

ROLE: CHASSIS, DRIVETRAIN + INTAKE · TOOLS: ONSHAPE, CNC

Chassis, swerve drivetrain and intake arm for the FRC Crescendo season. Custom structural and motion components were developed to meet competition weight and performance constraints, with full mechanical assemblies modeled to resolve drivetrain geometry, mounting structure and subsystem packaging.

  1. Swerve drivetrain — drivetrain geometry resolved in the full-assembly model; modules integrated into the chassis for precise, repeatable motion.
  2. Intake arm — custom structural and motion components designed for reliable game-piece pickup under match conditions.
  3. Fabrication + validation — CNC machining and rapid fabrication; drivetrain precision and intake reliability validated through iterative testing and competition.
