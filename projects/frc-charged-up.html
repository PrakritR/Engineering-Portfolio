Design Notes

ROLE: INTAKE ARM SUBSYSTEM · TOOLS: ONSHAPE, CNC

The intake arm subsystem for an 800+ part FRC competition robot, designed and delivered inside a six-week build season. The full intake assembly was modeled in CAD to define pivot geometry, load paths and mounting interfaces within strict weight and packaging constraints.

  1. Extension arm — pivot geometry defined in CAD to reach scoring positions while respecting frame-perimeter and packaging constraints.
  2. Pivot + load paths — loads resolved through the tower structure; mounting interfaces coordinated across an 800+ part robot assembly.
  3. End effector — fabricated with CNC and rapid prototyping; alignment, durability and repeatability validated through iterative testing and live competition.
