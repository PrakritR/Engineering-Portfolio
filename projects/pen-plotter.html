PR-002 — Pen Plotter

← BACK TO ALL PROJECTS

Design Notes

ROLE: DESIGNER + BUILDER · TOOLS: ONSHAPE, CNC, FDM

A rigid CoreXY motion platform with belt-driven linear actuation. Over 20 custom structural and motion components were engineered around one goal — positional accuracy — with frame stiffness, belt tension and calibration all tuned to cut backlash and improve repeatability.

  1. CoreXY belt drive — both steppers stay stationary and share the belt path, keeping moving mass low so acceleration doesn't excite the frame.
  2. Carriage + linear guides — linear rods and bearings selected to minimize deflection; frame stiffness and belt tension optimized for positional accuracy.
  3. Calibration — motion calibrated to reduce backlash and improve repeatability across the full work area.
